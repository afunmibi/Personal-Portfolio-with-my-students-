About Me!

A frontend web developer is responsible for creating the user interface and experience of a website or web application. This includes designing and coding the layout, navigation, and visual elements of the site using languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They also ensure that the site is responsive and works across different devices and browsers. Additionally, a frontend web developer may collaborate with designers, backend developers, and other stakeholders to ensure that the site meets the needs of the business or organization.
